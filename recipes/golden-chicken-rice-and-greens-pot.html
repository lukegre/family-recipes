Golden Chicken, Rice & Greens Pot

A gentle all-in-one meal for tired weeks: protein, carbs, greens and enough softness for leftovers.

Batch35 minOne-pot

Similar recipe

Ingredients

  • 600 g chicken thighs
  • 300 g brown rice or basmati
  • 3 carrots
  • 2 handfuls spinach or kale
  • 1 tsp turmeric, ginger and garlic
  • 200 g Greek yoghurt

Method

  1. Soften garlic, ginger and carrots with turmeric.
  2. Add rice, chicken and stock; simmer until rice is tender.
  3. Shred chicken, stir in greens, and serve with yoghurt.

Why it nourishes

Chicken, rice and greens give protein, gentle carbs and folate in one pot. Ginger and turmeric keep it warming without needing heat.

For your little one

For 10-month-old Keep stock low salt. Shred chicken finely, mash carrots, and chop greens small.
