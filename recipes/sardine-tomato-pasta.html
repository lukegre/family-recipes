Sardine Tomato Pasta

Fast, mineral-rich and surprisingly satisfying. Use tuna or salmon if sardines are a hard sell.

Recovery15 minOmega-3

Ingredients

  • 2 tins sardines
  • 500 ml passata or 300 g cherry tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 handfuls spinach
  • 400 g GF pasta
  • 1 lemon

Method

  1. Cook pasta while garlic and tomatoes simmer.
  2. Fold in sardines and spinach.
  3. Finish with lemon and olive oil.

Fast, mineral-rich and surprisingly satisfying. Use tuna or salmon if sardines are a hard sell.

For 10-month-old Check carefully for bones and choose low-salt fish if possible. Flake finely and cut pasta small.
