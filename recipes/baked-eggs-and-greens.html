Baked Eggs & Greens

Best fresh, but sauce base can be made ahead.

Recovery25 minProtein-rich

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 500 ml tomato base
  • 2 handfuls spinach
  • 1 handful herbs
  • 100 g optional feta

Method

  1. Warm tomato base in pan.
  2. Add greens.
  3. Crack in eggs and bake or cover until set.

Best fresh, but sauce base can be made ahead.

For 10-month-old Cook eggs fully until firm. Chop spinach finely. Serve with soft toast fingers or potato.
