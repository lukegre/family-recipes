Coconut Noodle Soup

Make only what you need; noodles are less good as leftovers.

Recovery20 minComforting

Ingredients

  • 250 g rice noodles
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • 700 ml stock
  • 300 g frozen vegetables
  • 400 g tofu, chicken or prawns

Method

  1. Simmer coconut milk and stock.
  2. Add protein and veg.
  3. Add rice noodles at the end.

Make only what you need; noodles are less good as leftovers.

For 10-month-old Cut noodles short. Keep broth low salt and temperature safe; offer soft veg/protein pieces.
