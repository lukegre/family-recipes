Baby-Friendly Chicken a la King

A mild creamy chicken and veg dinner that works over rice, potatoes or toast fingers.

Batch30 minBaby-friendly

Ingredients

  • 500 g chicken thighs or breast
  • 2 carrots
  • 200 g mushrooms
  • 150 g frozen peas
  • 300 ml whole milk
  • 200 ml low-salt chicken stock
  • 2 tbsp flour or cornflour
  • 300 g rice, potatoes or toast

Method

  1. Poach or pan-cook the chicken until cooked through, then rest briefly and shred finely.
  2. Dice carrots small and slice mushrooms thinly. Soften them in a little butter or olive oil for 8-10 minutes so they become tender enough for the baby portion.
  3. Whisk flour or cornflour with a splash of milk, then add the remaining milk and low-salt stock. Simmer until the sauce thickens and the carrots are soft.
  4. Stir in frozen peas and shredded chicken for the final few minutes. Season the adult portion at the table with salt, pepper, herbs or mustard.
  5. Serve over rice, mashed potato, baked potato or toast fingers. Loosen leftovers with milk when reheating.

Why it nourishes

Chicken brings protein and iron, milk adds calcium and energy, and peas, carrots and mushrooms make the creamy sauce more balanced.

For your little one

For 10-month-old Keep the sauce unsalted and skip stock cubes unless low-salt. Shred chicken finely, chop mushrooms small, squash peas, and serve with soft rice or potato.
