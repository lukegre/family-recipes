Saucy Gnocchi

Key rule: keep it saucy; dry gnocchi is poor without a microwave.

Flexible30 minComfort

Similar recipe

Ingredients

  • 600 g gnocchi
  • 500 g tomato or pesto sauce
  • 2 handfuls spinach
  • 250 g optional sausage, beans or feta

Method

  1. Cook gnocchi.
  2. Heat sauce with greens and protein.
  3. Combine generously.

Why it nourishes

Gnocchi covers the comfort-carb role. Adding spinach plus sausage, beans or feta turns it from a carb bowl into a more sustaining dinner.

For your little one

For 10-month-old Cut gnocchi into small pieces. Keep sauce mild and not too salty.

Leftovers / reheating

Key rule: keep it saucy; dry gnocchi is poor without a microwave.
