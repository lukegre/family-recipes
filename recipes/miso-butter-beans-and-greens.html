Miso Butter Beans & Greens

Creamy, savoury and almost no chopping. Good when you need real food quickly.

Recovery12 minPantry

Ingredients

  • 2 cans butter beans
  • 1 tbsp white miso
  • 2 handfuls spinach or chard
  • 2 tbsp butter or olive oil
  • 1 lemon
  • 300 g rice or 4 slices toast

Method

  1. Warm beans with a splash of water.
  2. Stir in miso, butter or oil, and greens.
  3. Serve over rice or toast with lemon.

Creamy, savoury and almost no chopping. Good when you need real food quickly.

For 10-month-old Miso is salty, so set aside beans before adding it. Squash beans and chop greens finely.
