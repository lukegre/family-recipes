Chicken, Sweet Potato & Broccoli Traybake

Reheat: covered in oven at 170°C. Good Monday anchor meal.

Batch40 minHands-off

Similar recipe

Ingredients

  • 600 g chicken thighs
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes
  • 1 head broccoli
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp tahini + lemon sauce

Method

  1. Cut sweet potato into even chunks and roast with olive oil for 15 minutes so it gets a head start.
  2. Add chicken thighs, season the adult portion, and roast until the chicken is cooked through and the potato is soft.
  3. Add broccoli for the final 10-12 minutes so it stays bright rather than scorched. Finish with a quick tahini, lemon and yoghurt sauce.

Why it nourishes

Chicken gives complete protein while sweet potato adds slow-release carbs and beta-carotene. Broccoli brings vitamin C and folate.

For your little one

For 10-month-old Offer soft shredded chicken, mashed sweet potato, and very soft broccoli florets. Cut into safe finger-food sizes.
