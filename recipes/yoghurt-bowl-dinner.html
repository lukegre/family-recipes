Yoghurt Bowl Dinner

Best as an emergency adult meal, not the main family default.

Fallback5 minEmergency

Ingredients

  • 600 g Greek yoghurt
  • 4 tbsp ground nuts or seeds
  • 300 g berries or banana
  • 4 tbsp nut butter
  • 1 tbsp honey if needed

Method

  1. Assemble in bowls.
  2. Add extra nuts/seeds for staying power.

Best as an emergency adult meal, not the main family default.

For 10-month-old Use plain full-fat yoghurt. Avoid whole nuts; use smooth nut butter or ground seeds.
