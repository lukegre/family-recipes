Thai Curry

Make it saucy so leftovers reheat well.

Flexible25 minFamily favourite

Similar recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp curry paste
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • 600 g vegetables
  • 500 g chicken, tofu or chickpeas
  • 300 g rice

Method

  1. Fry curry paste briefly in a little oil, then add coconut milk and stir until it becomes a smooth sauce.
  2. Add chicken, tofu or chickpeas with the vegetables that need longest cooking first. Simmer gently until tender.
  3. Finish with lime or lemon and serve over rice. Keep a mild baby portion aside before adding extra paste or chilli.

Why it nourishes

Coconut milk and rice make it comforting, while vegetables plus chicken, tofu or chickpeas cover protein and fibre. Keep plenty of veg in the pan.

For your little one

For 10-month-old Keep baby portion mild before adding extra curry paste or chilli. Serve rice with soft veg and shredded protein.

Leftovers / reheating

Make it saucy so leftovers reheat well.
