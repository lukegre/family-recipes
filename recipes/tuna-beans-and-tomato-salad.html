Tuna, Beans & Tomato Salad

High protein, high convenience. Not toddler-first, but excellent adult fallback.

Fallback8 minNo-cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tins tuna
  • 2 cans white beans
  • 300 g tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon

Method

  1. Mix everything.
  2. Serve with bread, potatoes or rice cakes.

High protein, high convenience. Not toddler-first, but excellent adult fallback.

For 10-month-old Use tuna occasionally and choose low-salt if possible. Squash beans and cut tomato small.
