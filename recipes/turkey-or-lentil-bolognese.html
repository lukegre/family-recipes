Turkey or Lentil Bolognese

Reheat: stovetop with splash water. Make double and freeze sauce.

Batch35 minFreezer-friendly

Ingredients

  • 500 g turkey mince or 250 g red lentils
  • 700 ml passata or canned tomatoes
  • 1 carrot, 1 celery stick, 1 onion
  • 1 tbsp Italian herbs
  • 400 g GF pasta or potatoes

Method

  1. Cook the veg base slowly if you can.
  2. Add mince or lentils and tomatoes.
  3. Simmer until rich.

Reheat: stovetop with splash water. Make double and freeze sauce.

For 10-month-old Keep baby portion low salt. Serve sauce with small pasta, potato, or soft veg; mince should be finely broken up.
