Baked Potato Bar

Hands-off and child-friendly. Good weekend option.

Flexible10 + 45 minLow effort

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 2 cans beans or tuna
  • 150 g cheese
  • 200 g yoghurt or sour cream
  • 300 g salad bits

Method

  1. Bake potatoes until soft.
  2. Put toppings on the table.
  3. Everyone builds their own.

Hands-off and child-friendly. Good weekend option.

For 10-month-old Serve mashed potato with yoghurt, soft beans, or cheese in small amounts. Avoid tough potato skin.
