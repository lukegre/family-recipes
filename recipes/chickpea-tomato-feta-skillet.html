Chickpea Tomato Feta Skillet

A strong Thursday meal: warm, fast, and forgiving.

Recovery20 minLow effort

Ingredients

  • 2 cans chickpeas
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 150 g feta
  • 1 handful herbs

Method

  1. Simmer tomatoes, garlic and chickpeas.
  2. Add feta at the end.
  3. Serve with rice, potatoes or GF bread.

A strong Thursday meal: warm, fast, and forgiving.

For 10-month-old Squash chickpeas flat to reduce choking risk. Keep feta minimal because it is salty.
