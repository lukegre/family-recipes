Red Pesto Beans on Toast

Very good Thursday option: minimal chopping, high satisfaction.

Recovery12 minPantry meal

Similar recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cans white beans
  • 4 tbsp red pesto
  • 2 handfuls spinach
  • 4 slices GF toast
  • 50 g parmesan or feta

Method

  1. Warm beans with pesto and splash water.
  2. Wilt in spinach.
  3. Serve on toast with cheese.

Why it nourishes

White beans are the quiet hero here: fibre, plant protein and minerals. Spinach and cheese round it out quickly.

For your little one

For 10-month-old Use pesto sparingly because it can be salty. Squash beans and serve on soft toast strips.

Leftovers / reheating
