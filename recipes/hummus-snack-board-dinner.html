Hummus Snack-Board Dinner

For evenings when cooking would tip the whole day over. Still balanced enough to count.

Fallback7 minNo-cook

Ingredients

  • 300 g hummus
  • 4 boiled eggs or 400 g cooked chicken
  • 2 avocados
  • 250 g cherry tomatoes
  • 1 pack GF crackers or toast
  • 400 g fruit

Method

  1. Put hummus, protein, carbs, fruit and veg on a board or plates.
  2. Add olive oil, lemon or herbs if you have them.

For evenings when cooking would tip the whole day over. Still balanced enough to count.

For 10-month-old Spread hummus thinly, quarter tomatoes lengthwise, and cut egg or chicken into safe soft pieces.
