Thermomix Carrot, Ginger & Red Lentil Soup

Good as dinner with eggs, toast or cheese; excellent as a lunch thermos the next day.

Batch25 minThermomix

Similar Thermomix recipe

Ingredients

  • 600 g carrots
  • 200 g red lentils
  • 2 cm ginger
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • 700 ml stock
  • 1 lime or lemon

Method

  1. Chop carrot, onion and ginger, then saute briefly with olive oil until fragrant.
  2. Add rinsed red lentils, stock and coconut milk. Cook until the carrots and lentils are very soft.
  3. Blend until smooth, then finish with lemon or lime. Add yoghurt, eggs or toast alongside if it needs to be a fuller dinner.

Why it nourishes

Red lentils add protein and iron, carrots bring beta-carotene, and coconut milk makes it satisfying enough for tired evenings.

For your little one

For 10-month-old Keep ginger gentle and stock low salt. Serve thick on pre-loaded spoons with yoghurt to cool.

Leftovers / reheating

Good as dinner with eggs, toast or cheese; excellent as a lunch thermos the next day.
