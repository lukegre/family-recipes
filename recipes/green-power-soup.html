Green Power Soup

Reheat: Thermomix or stovetop. Add bread, eggs or cheese for extra protein.

Recovery20 minThermomix

Ingredients

  • 2 courgettes
  • 250 g frozen peas
  • 2 handfuls spinach
  • 1 can white beans
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Cook vegetables and beans with stock.
  2. Blend smooth.
  3. Top with olive oil, seeds or cheese.

Reheat: Thermomix or stovetop. Add bread, eggs or cheese for extra protein.

For 10-month-old Serve thick in a small bowl or on pre-loaded spoons. Add yoghurt to cool it quickly.
