Halloumi Veg Bowls

Best fresh, but components work well for lunch bowls.

Flexible25 minVegetarian

Similar recipe

Ingredients

  • 250 g halloumi
  • 600 g courgette or peppers
  • 300 g rice or quinoa
  • 200 g hummus
  • 1 lemon

Method

  1. Pan-fry halloumi.
  2. Cook or roast veg.
  3. Serve with grains and hummus.

Why it nourishes

Halloumi gives quick protein and satisfaction, while grains, veg and hummus add fibre, colour and slower energy.

For your little one

For 10-month-old Halloumi is salty, so offer only a little. Focus on soft veg, hummus and grains.

Leftovers / reheating

Best fresh, but components work well for lunch bowls.
