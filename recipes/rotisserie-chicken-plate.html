Rotisserie Chicken Plate

Good to keep in mind for genuinely depleted days.

Fallback10 minNo-cook

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-cooked chicken
  • 1 bag salad
  • 2 avocados
  • 4 rice cakes, GF bread slices or potatoes
  • 200 g yoghurt sauce

Method

  1. Put everything on plates.
  2. Add sauce and olive oil.

Good to keep in mind for genuinely depleted days.

For 10-month-old Check salt level and remove skin. Shred chicken finely; serve with avocado and soft carb.
