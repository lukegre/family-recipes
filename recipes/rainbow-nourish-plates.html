Rainbow Nourish Plates

Very much in the gentle, abundant bowl-meal direction: flexible, colourful and leftover-friendly.

Flexible20 minBuild-your-own

Ingredients

  • 300 g cooked quinoa or rice
  • 600 g roasted or raw vegetables
  • 500 g eggs, chicken, tofu or beans
  • 2 avocados
  • 4 tbsp ground seeds
  • 200 g yoghurt herb sauce

Method

  1. Put grain, protein, veg and sauce on the table.
  2. Let everyone assemble their own plate.
  3. Keep extra components for lunch bowls.

Very much in the gentle, abundant bowl-meal direction: flexible, colourful and leftover-friendly.

For 10-month-old Serve deconstructed with soft veg, mashed avocado, squashed beans or shredded protein. Avoid whole seeds.
