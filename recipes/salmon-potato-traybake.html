Salmon Potato Traybake

Best fresh. Leftovers work cold/room temp as a lunch bowl.

Batch35 minOmega-3

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 800 g baby potatoes
  • 400 g green beans or broccoli
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Roast potatoes first.
  2. Add salmon and greens for the last 12–15 min.
  3. Finish with lemon and olive oil.

Best fresh. Leftovers work cold/room temp as a lunch bowl.

For 10-month-old Check carefully for bones. Flake salmon; serve with mashed potato and soft green beans.
