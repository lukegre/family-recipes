Tacos / Wraps

Good Saturday meal. Also useful for using up leftovers.

Flexible15 minAssemble

Similar recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 GF wraps or corn tortillas
  • 500 g beans, mince or chicken
  • 2 avocados
  • 250 g tomato or salsa
  • 200 g yoghurt sauce

Method

  1. Warm beans, mince or shredded chicken with mild spices and a splash of water so the filling stays soft.
  2. Set out tortillas or wraps with avocado, tomato or salsa, yoghurt sauce and any leftover vegetables.
  3. Let everyone build their own, keeping the baby plate deconstructed with soft fillings and no hard raw pieces.

Why it nourishes

This is a flexible protein-plus-veg meal: beans add fibre, avocado adds healthy fats, and yoghurt sauce gives protein and calcium.

For your little one

For 10-month-old Serve fillings separately: avocado, beans squashed flat, soft veg, shredded chicken. Avoid hard raw pieces.
