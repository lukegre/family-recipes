Quinoa Loaded Bowl

Tip: keep sauce separate; eat warm or room temperature.

Batch25 minModular

Similar recipe

Ingredients

  • 250 g quinoa
  • 600 g roasted vegetables
  • 1 can chickpeas or 150 g feta
  • 2 handfuls greens
  • 4 tbsp olive oil or yoghurt sauce

Method

  1. Cook quinoa.
  2. Roast vegetables.
  3. Serve with protein and sauce.

Why it nourishes

Quinoa, chickpeas or feta, greens and sauce make a balanced bowl with protein, fibre, minerals and enough fat to keep it satisfying.

For your little one

For 10-month-old Serve deconstructed: soft quinoa, mashed chickpeas, roasted veg strips. Go easy on salty feta.

Leftovers / reheating

Tip: keep sauce separate; eat warm or room temperature.
