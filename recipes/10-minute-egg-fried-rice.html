10-minute Egg Fried Rice

Keep frozen veg and eggs stocked at all times.

Fallback10 minBad-day meal

Ingredients

  • 500 g cooked rice
  • 4 eggs
  • 300 g frozen peas or vegetables
  • 2 tbsp tamari
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil if available

Method

  1. Scramble eggs.
  2. Add rice and vegetables.
  3. Season with tamari.

Keep frozen veg and eggs stocked at all times.

For 10-month-old Use low-salt tamari or leave seasoning out of baby portion. Ensure peas/veg are soft; egg fully cooked.
