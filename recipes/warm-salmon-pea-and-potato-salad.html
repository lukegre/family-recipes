Warm Salmon, Pea & Potato Salad

Works warm for dinner and cold for lunch, which is useful when reheating is awkward.

Flexible25 minFresh

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 800 g baby potatoes
  • 250 g frozen peas
  • 1 cucumber or 2 handfuls greens
  • 200 g Greek yoghurt
  • 1 handful dill and 1 lemon

Method

  1. Boil potatoes, adding peas near the end.
  2. Pan-cook or bake salmon.
  3. Toss with yoghurt, dill, lemon and greens.

Works warm for dinner and cold for lunch, which is useful when reheating is awkward.

For 10-month-old Check salmon for bones, flake well, and squash peas. Serve cucumber only if soft or finely chopped.
