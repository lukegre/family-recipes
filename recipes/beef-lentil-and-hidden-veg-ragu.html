Beef, Lentil & Hidden Veg Ragu

Make a double batch. It freezes well and brings iron, zinc and fibre without feeling worthy.

Batch40 minIron-rich

Ingredients

  • 500 g beef mince
  • 150 g red lentils
  • 700 ml passata
  • 1 carrot, 1 courgette, 1 onion
  • 1 tbsp Italian herbs
  • 400 g GF pasta or polenta

Method

  1. Cook onion, grated carrot and courgette until soft.
  2. Add beef, lentils, passata and herbs.
  3. Simmer until rich; loosen with water if needed.

Make a double batch. It freezes well and brings iron, zinc and fibre without feeling worthy.

For 10-month-old Break mince up finely and keep salt out. Serve with small pasta, potato, polenta or soft veg.
