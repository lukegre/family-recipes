Freezer Soup Upgrade

Turns a light soup into a proper dinner.

Fallback10 minEmergency

Ingredients

  • 1 litre frozen soup
  • 1 can white beans or lentils
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 tbsp seeds or cheese
  • 4 slices GF bread

Method

  1. Reheat soup on stove.
  2. Add beans or lentils.
  3. Top with oil, seeds or cheese.

Turns a light soup into a proper dinner.

For 10-month-old Keep baby portion low salt. Blend beans/lentils into soup or mash well.
