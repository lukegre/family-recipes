Omelette Plate

Not fancy, but ideal when the household is tired.

Recovery15 minProtein-first

Similar recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 100 g cheese or feta
  • 2 handfuls spinach
  • 250 g cherry tomatoes
  • 2 avocados, bread or potatoes

Method

  1. Make a simple omelette.
  2. Add greens and cheese.
  3. Serve with raw veg and a carb.

Why it nourishes

Eggs are nutrient-dense and fast, with protein, choline and iodine. Add avocado, potatoes or bread so it feels like dinner.

For your little one

For 10-month-old Cook egg through. Cut into strips; serve with soft avocado or tomato pieces.

Leftovers / reheating
