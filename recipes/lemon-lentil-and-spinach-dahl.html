Lemon Lentil & Spinach Dahl

Reheat: stovetop with a splash of water. Excellent for lunch leftovers.

Batch25 minThermomix-friendly

Ingredients

  • 200 g red lentils
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp curry spices
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • 2 handfuls spinach
  • 1 lemon

Method

  1. Cook onion, garlic and spices.
  2. Add lentils, coconut milk and water or stock.
  3. Simmer until soft, then stir in spinach and lemon.

Reheat: stovetop with a splash of water. Excellent for lunch leftovers.

For 10-month-old Keep spices mild. Mash or blend a portion; serve with rice. Avoid adding salt to the baby portion.
